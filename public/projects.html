RARASPACE — EMPLOYEE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Rara Digital Lab Pvt. Ltd.  ·  UI/UX Designer  ·  2022–2024

A comprehensive web application unifying employee management, task tracking, payroll, project management, leave systems, and inventory into one seamless platform — eliminating fragmented tools across a 12-person team.

Problem No unified system — teams lost time switching between disconnected tools, causing errors and communication gaps.
Process Google surveys → Pain point analysis → Paper sketches for 8+ screens → Figma prototyping → 3 rounds of usability testing with real users.

INTELLIX — HEALTHCARE KPI DASHBOARD

Rara Digital Lab Pvt. Ltd.  ·  UI/UX Designer  ·  2022–2024

Designed the KPI module and Patient Dashboard for a healthcare intelligence platform used daily by doctors and nurses in live hospital settings — transforming fragmented clinical data into clear, actionable performance insights.

Problem No centralized way to track KPIs — patient length of stay, checklist completion, and safety data were invisible to administrators.
Process Secondary research → Competition analysis → Direct feedback from doctors & nurses → 14-screen prototype → Stakeholder review cycles → Live analytics monitoring.
Impact 30% user satisfaction improvement. Reduced clinical cognitive load. KPI module adopted across hospital departments.

LOKSEWA PRO — EXAM PREP APP

Pisces Moon  ·  UI/UX Designer  ·  2024–2025

Designed a mobile app for Nepal's most competitive civil service exam — consolidating practice sets, quizzes, audio books, progress tracking, and offline access into one beautifully crafted experience. Became the leading Loksewa prep app in Nepal.

Problem Every existing Loksewa app failed users — outdated content, no progress tracking, poor navigation, and no reliable offline access.
Process App review analysis → Aspirant surveys → Competitor audit → Mid-fidelity wireframes → Dark & light theme design → Stakeholder review → Play Store launch.
Impact 10,000+ downloads and 4.4-star rating on Play Store. Praised by users for clean UI, organized content, and dark mode.
